Top Attractions in Kolhapur

Mahalaxmi Temple

An iconic spiritual site dedicated to Goddess Mahalaxmi, built in the 7th century.

Rankala Lake

A scenic lake surrounded by gardens, great for evening walks and boating.

Panhala Fort

A historic fort that tells tales of Shivaji Maharaj’s bravery and strategic genius.

New Palace Museum

Royal residence with Indo-Saracenic architecture, now a museum displaying artifacts.

Jyotiba Temple

Located on a hilltop, this temple offers panoramic views and vibrant festivals.
