<h1>How to Reach Kolhapur</h1>

<div class="section">
  <h2>🚆 By Train</h2>
  <p>Kolhapur Railway Station (Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Terminus) is well-connected to major cities like Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore, Hyderabad and Delhi. You can get direct trains like Sahyadri Express, Koyna Express, and Mahalaxmi Express.</p>
</div>

<div class="section">
  <h2>🚍 By Bus</h2>
  <p>MSRTC (ST buses) and private buses run daily from Pune, Mumbai, Satara, and Sangli. Buses are available 24/7 from Kolhapur Central Bus Stand.</p>
</div>

<div class="section">
  <h2>✈️ By Air</h2>
  <p>Kolhapur Airport (Ujlaiwadi) is 10 km from the city and has flights to Mumbai and Bangalore. Nearest major airport is Pune International Airport (~230 km).</p>
</div>
