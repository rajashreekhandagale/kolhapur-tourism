Famous Kolhapuri Food & Shopping

Misal Pav

Spicy and crunchy, Kolhapuri Misal is a popular street food served with pav, farsan and rassa.

Tambda Rassa

A fiery red mutton curry made with Kolhapuri spices — bold and flavorful!

Kolhapuri Bhadang

Spicy puffed rice snack mixed with peanuts, garlic and masala — perfect for tea time.

Kolhapuri Chappals

Handmade leather slippers known for durability and style — a must-buy from Kolhapur!

Kolhapuri Jaggery (Gul)

Organic jaggery made from sugarcane — sweet and healthy souvenir from the city.
